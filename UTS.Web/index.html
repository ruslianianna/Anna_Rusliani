Saloupa Waterfall

Natural beauty Saluopa Waterfall has 12 levels that form a small pool, so the natural panorama is like spreading flowers. This waterfall is also surrounded by beautiful and fresh tropical forests. There are Several Indigenous Tribes in Poso,Central Sulawesi
namely:

Pamona Tribe: A tribe spread across several areas in Poso Regency. The Pamona language, also known as the Poso language, is spoken by around 200,000 people of the Pamona tribe. Lore Tribe: Tribe inhabiting parts of Poso Regency. Mori Tribe: A tribe that occupies three sub-districts in Poso Regency, namely Mori Atas, Petasio and Lembo Sub-districts. Apart from native tribes, in Poso there are also immigrant tribes, such as Bugis, Makasar, Toraja, Minahasa, Gorontalo, Arab, Batak and Javanese. These diverse tribes live together in Poso to form a multicultural society. Sintuwu Maroso's cultural philosophy means that strong togetherness can unite diverse tribal, cultural and ethnic backgrounds.
