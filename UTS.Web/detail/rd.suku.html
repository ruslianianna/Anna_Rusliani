Further explanation about the Pamona, Mori, Lore tribes and other migrant tribes

The following is an explanation of the Pamona, Mori and Lore tribes in Poso Regency, Central Sulawesi: Pamona Tribe The Pamona tribe inhabits almost all of Poso Regency, part of Tojo Una-Una Regency, and part of North Morowali Regency. The Pamona tribe is also in East Luwu Regency, South Sulawesi. The Pamona tribe is also known as the Poso, Bare'e, or To Pamona tribe. Mori Tribe: The Mori tribe is one of the tribes in Poso Regency. Lore Tribe: The Lore tribe is one of the tribes in Poso Regency. Poso Coastal Tribe The Poso Pesisir tribe is a tribe formed from a mixture of the Pamona, Lage and Kaili tribes with migrant tribes, especially the Bugis. Pamona language The Pamona language has many syllable phases that can be rotated to form different meanings. The Pamona language faces serious challenges because many other languages ​​are used by the community, especially the younger generation. Religion The majority of Poso Regency residents are Christian, but the majority of Poso city residents are Muslim.V
