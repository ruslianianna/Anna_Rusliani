The Traditional Clothes of the Poso area include,FUYA

Cultural heritage of Poso district, FUYA traditional clothing from the BADA tribe in the highlands of South Lore and West Lore is made of BEA bark. The way it is made is by peeling the bark from the tree then soaking it for a few days then drying it after drying the bark is beaten using a special tool according to the desired shape and size for coloring still using bark sap.
