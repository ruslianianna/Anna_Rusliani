Typical Poso Regional Food, INUYU

Bamboo rice is a food made from sticky rice and coconut milk cooked in bamboo. The following is a complete explanation of bamboo rice: Ingredients Bamboo rice is made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, banana leaves and bamboo. How to make The bamboo is cleaned, then banana leaves are put into it. Rice mixed with coconut milk is put into bamboo. After that, the bamboo is burned over a fire until it turns black. Cooking To cook bamboo rice, you should use medium-sized bamboo and cover it with banana leaves so that the rice doesn't stick. When burning, make sure the fire is adjusted to the height of the bamboo so that the rice cooks evenly. Don't forget to turn the bamboo so that the sticky rice is cooked perfectly. Serving Bamboo rice is usually served with side dishes such as vegetables, fish or meat. Benefit Bamboo rice contains vitamin B6 which can help protect teeth from damage, strengthen teeth and prevent caries. Bamboo rice also contains phosphorus which can help relieve respiratory symptoms such as irritated coughs and sore throats. Other name Bamboo rice is also known as lemang, inuyu, or cani rice.
